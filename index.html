Publications and research

Manuscript in preparation — IEEE TPS 2026

ML-DTEM: Machine Learning Based Dynamic Trust Evaluation Model for Blockchain-Enabled Zero Trust IAM Systems

Proposes an ensemble ML framework combining XGBoost, LSTM, and Isolation Forest with Hyperledger Fabric blockchain for continuous Zero Trust trust evaluation in enterprise IAM. Achieves 97.4% classification accuracy, 1.8% false positive rate, and sub-200ms end-to-end latency on a 500,000-event synthetic enterprise dataset. Outperforms rule-based Zero Trust baseline (78.3%, 12.4% FPR) and all single-model approaches.

Understanding the Limitations of AI Chatbots in Enterprise Environments: A Critical Analysis

Examines six core limitation domains of enterprise AI chatbot deployments — personalisation deficiencies, contextual understanding failures, inadequate problem-solving, data privacy vulnerabilities, legacy integration barriers, and predefined response rigidity. Proposes the Hybrid Adaptive Chatbot Architecture (HACA) framework with four foundational pillars.
