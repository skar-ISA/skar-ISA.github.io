About

I began my career in cloud infrastructure, joining PHD Engineers as a Junior Azure Engineer in 2021 and progressively taking on multi-cloud migration and DevOps responsibilities. Over four years, I engineered infrastructure across GCP, Azure, and AWS — designing migration strategies, implementing CI/CD pipelines with Terraform and GitHub Actions, and managing identity security at enterprise scale.

At FIS Global, I served as IAM Subject Matter Expert for American Express — managing access governance for over 10,000 users, maintaining 98% SLA adherence, and being recognised as Best Employee Performer in both 2024 and 2025 by the American Express client.

Since April 2025, I have been teaching Computer Science and Informatics Practices to Classes 5–12 at Vidyanjali Coaching Institute and through private tuitions — applying the same rigour I brought to enterprise operations to structured academic instruction.

I am currently pursuing MCA specialising in Blockchain & Cybersecurity at MRIIRS, preparing for UGC NET 2026, and building AI automation projects. My research, published in 2024, examines the architectural and governance limitations of enterprise AI chatbot deployments.
